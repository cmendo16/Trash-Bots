About TrashBots

                 TrashBots merges environmental advocacy with cutting-edge technology. Our mission is to 
                 empower individuals and communities to combat litter and build a sustainable future.


                 Driven by a team passionate about leveraging AI for good, our web application simplifies 
                 reporting litter and connecting with fellow environmental enthusiasts.

        
                 Join us in our mission to make a difference. With TrashBots, 
                 taking action for a cleaner planet has never been easier or more rewarding.


                 TrashBots foster community engagemenet by encouraging users to participate in cleanup 
                 efforts, organize events, and collaborate with others on the platform.

TrashBots Unveiled

Litter Detection

                        TrashBots are equipped with advanced AI image recognition to detect and identify
                        littler in the environment.

Reporting and Logging

                        TrashBots automatically generate reports detailing the location, type, quantity of litter found, 
                        making it easy for users to contribute to cleanup efforts.

Guidance and Assistance

                        Through interactive prompts, trashbots guide users through the steps of reporting
                        litter sitings, uploading photos, and providing additonal details. 
                        
                        TrashBots collect and analyze data on littering patterns and behaviors, providing valuable insights to 
                        local authorities, organizations, and policymakers.

Incentives and Rewards

                        Users earn points for reporting litter, participating in cleanup events, and engaging 
                        with other users on the platform, which can be redeemed for exclusive rewards such as 
                        merchandise, discounts, or donations to environmental causes.
